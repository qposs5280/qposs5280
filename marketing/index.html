World Affairs Challenge

The World Affairs Challenge is a group that seeks to make a positive difference in the world, starting with students in middle and high schoolers. Some of their goals are:

  • Research and work with subject matter expers to learn about the root causes of a specific global issue, and how it impacts communities around the world
  • Implement community service projects that contribute toward solving the issue
  • Student teams present their work to a panel of judges for feedback and scoring
